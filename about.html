    Hey there, my name is Tom Eccles and I’m a student at Capilano University who loves taking photos of birds and long walks in the woods. I’m currently majoring in the Interaction Design Diploma program.

    I’m incredibly passionate about how the work I design for UX and UI can make experiences more thoughtful, intuitive, and enjoyable for users.
