Richmond Nightmarket Redesign

This project focuses on a redesign of the Richmond Night Market website, aiming to better capture the energy, culture, and identity that define the experience itself. The Richmond Night Market is rooted in vibrant Asian street market traditions, blending food, entertainment, and community into a uniquely immersive nightlife destination. My goal was to translate those brand values—liveliness, cultural richness, and sensory overload—into a cohesive digital experience.

I approached the redesign by leaning into the theme of nightlife: bold contrasts, glowing elements, and a sense of movement throughout the interface. Drawing inspiration from rave posters and acidic design, I incorporated high-contrast color palettes, experimental typography, and dynamic layouts to evoke the excitement and chaos of the market at night. These visual choices aim to mirror the feeling of walking through crowded aisles filled with neon lights, music, and the constant buzz of activity.

The result is a modern, immersive redesign that reflects both the cultural roots and high-energy atmosphere of the Richmond Night Market. It bridges expressive, experimental design with practical usability, creating a website that not only informs users but also captures the spirit of the experience before they even arrive.

One of my main goals was to balance this expressive style with usability. The original site lacked clarity and structure, so I focused on a more intuitive layout, clearer navigation, and better content hierarchy.

Most importantly for users, Key information such as vendors, events, and operation hours is now easier to find, while still being embedded within a visually engaging system.
