Crossroads Jazz Club Cafe

Crossroads is a concept website design for a jazz club café and bar located in Downtown Vancouver. The project focuses on creating a clean, professional, and highly accessible WordPress site that reflects the relaxed sophistication of a live jazz venue while remaining simple and easy to navigate.

The design embraces a minimal, sans-serif aesthetic, using clear typography, balanced spacing, and a restrained color palette to ensure readability across all devices. Rather than overwhelming users with visual complexity, the layout prioritizes function—making it easy to find essential information such as live performance schedules, menus, hours, and reservations. The structure is intuitive, guiding visitors seamlessly from discovering upcoming jazz acts to exploring the café and bar offerings.

Live Music & Quality Dining

My aim was to subtly capture the atmosphere of Crossroads through curated imagery and thoughtful content placement. Live jazz performances are highlighted as the core experience, supported by a diverse menu that blends café-style dining with evening bar service.

Built within WordPress, the design also considers ease of maintenance, allowing staff to update events and menu items without friction.

Overall, the project delivers a polished and functional digital presence that aligns with the venue’s identity—inviting, refined, and centered around music, food, and accessibility.
